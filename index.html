OneHack

Onehack is a 24 hour hackathon conducted at RIT, Kottayam as part of RITU, National level Technocultural fest under Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell(IEDC)- RIT. This is the first edition of onehack coming up in 2018 which will be continued in the years later. Onehack is open to both men and women. Onehack will be an equal opportunity hackathon giving opportunities to women,men to prove their talents. Another major speciality of onehack is that we provide special focus and extra mentor support to students who don't have previous hackathon experiences.
